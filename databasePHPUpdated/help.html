What is Bangor Community?

  Bangor Community is an app that lets you participate in your local charities. Using coins earned through volunteering and walking, you can vote for the charities you want to recieve a monthly donation.

Quests

  Quests are given out by charities. By completing a quest, you can earn coins.

Getting Quests

  On your user page there is an add quest button and a drop down menu to choose which quests you want.

Changing your Charities

  By accessing your user page, you can change your charities and percentages at any time.
